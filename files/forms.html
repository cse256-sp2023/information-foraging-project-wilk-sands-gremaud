Enrollment

Forms

Once your application has been accepted, please fill out the following forms to enroll.


Enrollment Form - You must fill this form out every year that your child attends WUNS.

Developmental History Form - This form only needs to be filled out once.


The following forms are only available as PDFs and also must be filled out:

Child Medical Examination Report

Immunization Consent/History


Recently Accepted Students

There is a one-month tuition deposit that must be paid for children who have just been accepted at the Washington University Nursery School that will be credited for the school year.
